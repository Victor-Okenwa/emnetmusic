About Emnet

Ement is a music streaming platform developed by emark ultimate for the sole purpose of promoting young talented artists, band of musicians and instrumentalists. We help them to showcase their music across Africa and beyond.

Our goal is to make African musicians a world wide sensation and building a trust connection between our clients/customers and us.
